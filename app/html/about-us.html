Keeping people safe from extreme weathers...

Who we are

Our team BBY15 is developing Extremis to help people keep track of extreme weather conditions to protect themselves and their properties by following real-time weather conditions and by sharing their current situations.


How it works

We use the geolocation and the weather api to forecast future extreme weathers for people who are in constant threat. We also provide a posting function for people to share information and get through the extreme weather events safely together.

Our commitments

We believe that such an impactful application like ours should be easy to use for all people. Our goal is to deliver safety and relaxation to our many Extremis users. That means that our team will indefinitely work hard to deliver the most accurate forecasts out there. We will never give anyone up!
